Carne Con Chile Recipe

Description

Carne con chile is one of the most common, simple, cheap, yet delicious Mexican Recipes we can make. What makes it so amazing is how versatile it can be and how much novice cooks are able to experiment with it. We can have varying levels of spicyness and there are several types of meat we can use. This recipe will be the standard, common way of making it: Carne con Chile Verde and Milanesa.

Ingredients

  • 6 Chile Serrano
  • 3lb Milanesa
  • 3 Tomatoes
  • 2 Tomates
  • 1 Garlic
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Boil the other stuff
  3. Add salt and pepper to the meat
  4. Once stuff is boiled, blend in a blender to make salsa. Add salt
  5. Once meat is ready, add the salsa
  6. Heat until it boils. Enjoy!
